About

Twelve years spent moving between the studio, the stage and the headset — building worlds that fashion and culture can actually walk into.

Pat is a London-based Creative Director and Creative Technologist running the independent studio BRGB, working across large-scale installation, XR and real-time production. He was an early hire at Satore Studio, helping grow the team from a handful of people to thirty-two, and has directed spatial and social-first work for Adidas, Hugo Boss, Moschino and Prada.

Alongside the studio, he lectures in Digital Media at the University of East London, teaching game art, immersive media and creative technology to the next set of people who'll build this stuff.
